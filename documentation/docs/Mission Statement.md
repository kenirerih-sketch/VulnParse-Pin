# Mission Statement

VulnParse-Pin exists to make vulnerability triage open, explainable, and operationally scalable.

## Mission

Enable security teams to turn raw scanner output into trusted remediation decisions through transparent scoring, resilient parsing, and high-volume performance.

## Core values

- **Transparency:** Risk logic should be inspectable and auditable
- **Security:** Safe defaults should protect users and downstream systems
- **Performance:** Tooling should scale with enterprise finding volume
- **Practicality:** Outputs should be actionable for real remediation workflows
- **Extensibility:** Architecture should support new parsers and passes cleanly

## Governance intent

- Keep core behavior reproducible and test-backed
- Support community collaboration through open licensing
- Provide commercial pathways for organizations with proprietary constraints

## Success criteria

- Reduced triage time and improved prioritization fidelity
- Broader adoption by SOC and vulnerability management teams
- Strong contributor ecosystem and high-confidence regression coverage
- Clear compliance alignment for enterprise governance needs

## Commitment

VulnParse-Pin prioritizes correctness, clarity, and operational trust over opaque “black-box” scoring.
